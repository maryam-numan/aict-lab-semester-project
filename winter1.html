Primrose
Primroses are charming, colorful flowers that bloom in the spring. They have rounded petals and come in a variety of bright colors, including yellow, pink, red, blue, and white. These flowers are often found in gardens and flower beds because they add a splash of color early in the year. Primroses have green, crinkly leaves and grow close to the ground, making them a delightful sight in any garden.
"Primula spp."

Light
