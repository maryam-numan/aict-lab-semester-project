Tulip
Tulips are beautiful, colorful flowers that are shaped like a cup. They come in many different colors, like red, yellow, pink, purple, and even white. Tulips grow from bulbs, which are planted in the ground. In the spring, the tulip stems grow up, and the flowers bloom. They are very popular in gardens and also used in flower arrangements. People often associate tulips with spring because that's when they typically blossom.
" Tulipa spp."
