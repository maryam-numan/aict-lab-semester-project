Aster
Asters are lovely, daisy-like flowers that come in vibrant colors such as purple, pink, blue, and white. They have a yellow center and long, thin petals that create a star-like shape. Asters bloom in late summer to fall, adding a burst of color to gardens when many other flowers are fading. They attract butterflies and bees, making them a delightful addition to any garden.
"Aster spp."
Care Level
Light
Water
Toxicity
Aster are easy to care of.
Full sun to partial shade
Moderate; avoid waterlogging
They are mildly toxic to humans and pets if ingested.
