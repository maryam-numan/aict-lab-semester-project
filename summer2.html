Marigold
Marigolds are bright, cheerful flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. They have round, full blooms and a distinct smell. Marigolds are popular in gardens because they are easy to grow and help keep pests away. Their vibrant colors make them a favorite for adding a sunny touch to flower beds and pots.
"Tagetes spp."
Care Level
Light
Water
Toxicity
Marigold are easy to care of.
Full sun
Regular;allow soil to dry between waterings.
They are mildly toxic to humans and pets if ingested.
