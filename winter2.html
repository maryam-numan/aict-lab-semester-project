Winterberry
Winterberry is a type of holly bush known for its bright red berries that add a splash of color to winter landscapes. The berries appear in the fall and often stay on the plant through the winter, making it a festive addition to gardens. Winterberry bushes have simple, green leaves that drop off in the fall, leaving the colorful berries on bare branches. They are often used for decoration during the holiday season.
"llex verticillata"
