Lilac
lilac description in easy words Lilacs are beautiful, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. They grow in clusters of small, tube-shaped flowers that come in colors like purple, pink, white, and blue. Lilac bushes have green, heart-shaped leaves and are often planted in gardens because of their lovely scent and attractive appearance.
" Syringa spp."
Care Level
Light
Water
Toxicity
Lilacs are low-maintenance.
Full sun
Regular during growth;
They are non-toxic to humans and pets but can cause digestive issues in animals if consumed in large amounts.
